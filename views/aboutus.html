  2. Discovery of MOOC Courses

    June 13th, 2014

    In the summer of '14 I came across a post on r/learnprogramming titled "100+ programming courses you can start today for FREE". I couldn't believe it, up to that point my assumption was that the only way one could learn programming was to go to an accredited university and get a four year computer science degree. I was overwhelmed by the resources these MOOC courses provided, and this over exposure to material was something I struggled for a while. In took me a about year of trying out different online courses to find a direction I was comfortable with.
