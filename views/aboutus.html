  2. Discovery of MOOC Courses

    June 13th, 2014

    MOOC stands for Massive Online Open Courses. In the summer '14 I came across a post on r/learnprogramming titled "100+ programming courses you can start today for FREE". Up to that point it was my assumption was that the only way one could learn programming was to go to an accredited university and get a four year computer science degree. I was overwhelmed by the resources these MOOC courses provided, and this over exposure to material was something I struggled with for a while. In took me a about year of trying out different online courses to find a direction I was comfortable with.
